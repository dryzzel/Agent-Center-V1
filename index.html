CSV Format Guide

For the leads to display correctly, your CSV file must have the following headers (order doesn't matter):

  • Date Sent
  • Prev. Company
  • Name
  • Address
  • Phone
  • Email or Second Phone
  • Product
  • Prev. Status
  • Call Log
  • Call Dispo
  • Comments

Example:
Date Sent,Prev. Company,Name,Address,Phone,Email or Second Phone,Product,Prev. Status,Call Log,Call Dispo,Comments
2025-01-01,OldCo,Juan Perez,123 Main St,5551234567,juan@example.com,Windows,Interested,Called once,NA,Needs follow up
